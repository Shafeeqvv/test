Introduction to China’s Untapped Legal Market

Seize Opportunities in the World’s Second-Largest Economy

Despite China's massive economic scale, with a GDP representing 18% of global output, fewer than 200 foreign law firms currently operate here. This imbalance reveals an untapped potential for law firms to expand their influence in areas like cross-border M&A, outbound investment, and emerging sectors such as fintech. Cities like Shenzhen, with a GDP that rivals developed nations, remain underserved in international legal services, creating a fertile ground for firms seeking growth in China’s dynamic landscape.
Through the Swiss Chinese Law Association (SCLA), foreign firms can unlock these opportunities with a risk-free, zero-cost entry model. SCLA offers an unparalleled support system, from securing government-backed subsidies to building strategic alliances with Chinese businesses.

Why Partner with SCLA?

A Proven Partner for Expanding International Legal Influence in China

For law firms aiming to make their mark in China, SCLA is more than a facilitator—it’s a strategic ally. Our association, in collaboration with local authorities and real estate partners, provides:

Zero-Cost Market Entry

SCLA eliminates traditional barriers to entry, such as consulting fees, with zero-cost solutions, including setup support and funding assistance tailored for international law firms entering China’s competitive legal arena.

Government-Backed Subsidies

We help firms leverage financial incentives like rent subsidies, one-time rewards of up to $200,000, and streamlined regulatory support, empowering them to establish and grow their presence without heavy overhead costs.

Deeply-Rooted Local Networks

By bridging cultural and business gaps, SCLA offers direct access to a local network of government bodies, industry experts, and Chinese law firms, fostering invaluable partnerships that give your firm a competitive edge.

Comprehensive Services to Ensure Success

From Initial Setup to Long-Term Growth—Supporting Every Step of Your Journey

SCLA’s services cover every aspect of entering and thriving in China’s legal market:

Internal Recommendations & Fast-Track Approvals

SCLA leverages its network to secure pre-approval and expedite necessary paperwork, ensuring that law firms can navigate the complex regulatory landscape with minimal friction and a high success rate.

Document Preparation & Compliance

Our team expertly drafts and reviews essential documents, guiding firms through the setup of representative offices and the registration process with local tax, banking, and foreign exchange authorities.

Subsidy and Reward Application

We handle the complete process of applying for subsidies, including financial incentives for office spaces and participation in government-funded projects. With our support, firms receive timely access to their subsidies, maximizing their setup capital.

Business Development & Networking

SCLA’s Shenzhen Members Club, a 300+ square meter facility, is available for business development activities, offering an ideal venue for meetings, networking events, and professional gatherings. We also facilitate connections with local law firms, enhancing firms' ability to offer expanded services and gain local insights.

Ongoing Strategic Assistance

Beyond the setup, SCLA remains a long-term partner, providing ongoing business strategy consultations, support in bidding for government and corporate projects, and inclusion in supplier lists for key enterprises.
